Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 3-6 months, while commercial projects can range from 6-18 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

What is your design process?

Our process includes initial consultation, concept development, design refinement, construction documentation, and project management. We work closely with clients at every stage to ensure their vision is realized.

Do you work with existing contractors?

Yes, we can work with your preferred contractors or recommend trusted professionals from our network. We ensure all contractors meet our quality standards and can execute the design vision.

What are your fees and pricing structure?

Our fees vary based on project scope and services required. We offer consultation rates, design-only packages, and full-service options. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

Yes, we offer comprehensive 3D visualization services including photorealistic renderings, virtual reality tours, and animation videos to help you visualize your project before construction begins.
